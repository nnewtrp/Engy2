About Us

Engy is a web application of NGV bus in Thammasat University Rangsit Campus. This application will help you to solve the problem when you use NGV bus by showing the real-time location of NGV bus, selecting the bus route that you want to get on, and showing the station of that NGV bus route pass.

Developed by Energize Group
